When It Started

We began in the year 2012 as a small community to the help young youth to learn a few basic skills that would help them in day to day tasks. We further progressed to teaching them on how to develop their talents. We grew into a cooperation that started to give different workshops to youth country wide

Our Benefactors

We work together with institutions such as google, UNISEF, World bank,Together for good foundation,YouTube in hopes of attaining a stable monetary fund to facilitate our program and spread awareness about our program.
